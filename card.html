GALÁPAGOS PENGUIN

This is the only penguin species found north of the equator and in the Galápagos.

  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Spheniscus mendiculus
  • WEIGHT: 5.5 pounds
  • LENGTH: 19 inches long
  • HABITAT: Oceans

Galapagos penguin (Spheniscus mendiculus), characterized by the presence of a narrow C-shaped band of white feathers that extends from the eye to the chin on each side of the head and a single band of black feathers that cuts across the large region of white feathers on the breast. Population numbering no more than a few thousand individuals.
